## About this episode

Jamie Taylor swings by to school Mike on SpecKit. SpecKit is a toolkit that acts as a structured "middleman" or context-provider between a developer and an LLM. Rather than simply asking an AI to "build a feature," SpecKit enforces a disciplined process that treats specifications as part of the source code.
